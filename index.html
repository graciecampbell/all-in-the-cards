What is Anime?

Anime is hand-drawn and computer-generated animation originating from Japan.

The first anime was created in 1933. Titled Chikara to Onna no Yo no Naka, was a short film and inspired the whole anime genre.

Fast Facts

  • 1. Anime is highly inspired by Disney. With Disney's animated movies, lots of inspiration was found with films like Bambi or Mickey Mouse.
  • 2. Sazae-San is the longest running Anime of all time. It has over 7,400 episodes in the series.
  • 3. Anime is a subject to study in college in Japan. With how popular Anime is, it is offered as a major where students learn animation and business tactics.
